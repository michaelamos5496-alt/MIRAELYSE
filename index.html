MIRA ELYSE SCARVES

MIRA ELYSE Silks is a brand dedicated to preserving, reviving, and interpreting beautiful motifs found in South East Asia. Staying true to artisanal production, the constant quest for unique motifs and exceptional techniques create elegant and timeless pieces. Specializing in silks, MIRA ELYSE offers:

  • A variety of SCARVES and ACCESSORIES, perfect for gifting.
  • Five luxurious TRAVEL SETS; The MIRA ELYSE Essentials.
  • And a READY TO WEAR collection that personifies elegance in garments 

Independent and socially responsible, the company is committed to maintaining a zero-waste policy. Explore our definition of elegance with the highest quality, indulgent silks!
