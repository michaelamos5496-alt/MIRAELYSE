ABOUT MIRA ELYSE

The Brand.
Mira Elyse is a brand dedicated to pushing the boundaries in Jewellery making and in the design scarves, pocket square. We explore new frontiers in our design and craftsmanship by combining different kinds of beads such as Murano Glass beads, Semi Precious stones, Tagua nut beads, Swarovski crystal beads, Coral beads, Freshwater Pearls and other authentic rare beads in our jewellery designs creating peerless pieces. Traditional wax print motifs, kente patterns, contemporary and abstract designs to mention a few are some of the artwork we incorporate into our versatile scarves and pocket squares designs. We hold nothing back in our attempt to add a touch of colour and elegance to your style, and also making Mira Elyse accessories the perfect gift for a loved one. Mira Elyse is also in the process of launching a new product which will be a perfect piece of memento for any special occasion.

The Company.
Based in the United Kingdom and operating in Ghana, Mira Elyse is dedicated to sharing the rich artistic heritage of Ghana with the world. Our mission is to maintain the authenticity of Ghanaian motifs while thoughtfully blending global design influences.

The Designer.
Mira Elyse was founded by lady Agnes Essandoh. Her creative journey began when her daughter entered sixth form, giving her the time to rekindle a longstanding passion for fashion and design. Her love for colour, culture and craftsmanship now inspires each handcrafted jewellery piece, scarf pocket squares and accessory in the collection.
